Problem One

Determine the value of $k$ for which the system below has no solutions.

$ \left\{ \begin{array}{r@{}r@{}r@{}r@{}r} x & + y & + 4 z & = & 3 \\ x & +2y & - 3 z & = & -2 \\ 4 x & +10 y & + k z & = & -17 \end{array} \right. $

Solution

Augmented Matrix:
$ \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & 4 & | & 3 \\ 1 & 2 & -3 & | & -2 \\ 4 & 10 & k & | & -17 \end{bmatrix} $

Step 1: Multiply Row 1 by -2, then remove all negatives in Row 2:
$ \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & 4 & | & 3 \\ 1 & 0 & 11 & | & 8 \\ 4 & 10 & k & | & -17 \end{bmatrix} $

Step 2: Subtract Row 2 from Row 1:
$ \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 1 & -7 & | & -5 \\ 1 & 0 & 11 & | & 8 \\ 4 & 10 & k & | & -17 \end{bmatrix} $

Step 3: Subtract -4 times Row 2 from Row 3:
$ \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 1 & -7 & | & -5 \\ 1 & 0 & 11 & | & 8 \\ 0 & 10 & k - 44 & | & -49 \end{bmatrix} $

Step 4: Add -10 times Row 1 to Row 3:
$ \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 1 & -7 & | & -5 \\ 1 & 0 & 11 & | & 8 \\ 0 & 0 & k - 44 + 70 & | & 1 \end{bmatrix} $

Step 5: Now, solve for k so that the bottom row only has zeros:
$ k - 44 + 70 = 0 $
$ k + 26 = 0 $
$ k = -26 $

Therefore, $k = -26$
